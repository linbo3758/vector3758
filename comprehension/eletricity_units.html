Comprehension Dashboard
🔹 1 Square = 1 Coulomb of Charge (Q)
🔹 1 Gold Circle = 1 Joule of Energy (E)
🎯 Current (I = Q/t): How many Squares pass through the bulb per second?
👉 Current: 3 A (determined by Voltage / Resistance)
🎯 Voltage (V = E/Q): How many Gold Circles each square brings into the bulb?
👉 Voltage: 4 V (energy is completely transferred inside the bulb)
🎯 Power (P = I × V): Total energy transferred to the bulb per second.
👉 Power: 3 × 4 = 12 W
Circuit Variables
Adjust Parameters
Voltage (V) — Energy per Charge 4 V
Resistance (R) — Opposition to Flow 2 Ω
Resulting Current (I = V/R): 2.00 A
GCSE Formulas Reference
• I = Q / t  (Current = Charges passing per second)
• V = E / Q  (Voltage = Energy lost per charge in the bulb)
• I = V / R  (Ohm's Law: Resistance limits the flow rate)
• P = I × V  (Power = Total energy converted per second)
